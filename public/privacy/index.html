Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Scale Small AI ("we", "our", "us") operates the Scale Small AI social media management platform (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your social media accounts through our Service, we collect:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and business name provided during signup.
  • Social media tokens: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens for the platforms you connect (Facebook, Instagram, Google Business Profile, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, YouTube, TikTok). These tokens allow us to publish content on your behalf.
  • Page and profile data: Your Facebook Page ID, Instagram Business account ID, and similar identifiers needed to publish to your accounts.
  • Photos: Job site photos you upload or that are ingested from connected services (e.g., CompanyCam) for the purpose of creating social media posts.
  • Post history: Records of content we publish to your accounts, including captions, images, and platform post IDs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the Service:

  • To publish photos and captions to your connected social media accounts on your behalf.
  • To generate AI-written captions tailored to each platform.
  • To track posting history and avoid duplicate content.
  • To maintain and refresh your platform connections so the Service continues to work.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or social media data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS) with encryption at rest and in transit. OAuth tokens are stored in Supabase Vault with additional encryption. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with the following third-party platforms to provide its functionality:

  • Meta (Facebook & Instagram): We use the Facebook Graph API and Instagram Content Publishing API to publish posts to your Pages and accounts.
  • Google: We use the Google Business Profile API and YouTube Data API to publish to your business listing and channel.
  • OpenAI: We use OpenAI's API to generate captions. Your photos and business context (not personal data) are sent to OpenAI for caption generation.
  • Fal AI: We use Fal AI to render video content from your photos for YouTube Shorts and TikTok.

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing their use of data.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at admin@scalesmall.ai or by visiting our Data Deletion page. Upon receiving a deletion request, we will remove your data within 30 days, including revoking all stored OAuth tokens.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Disconnect your social media accounts at any time through the Service dashboard or by revoking access directly on each platform.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
