Data Deletion

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Scale Small AI respects your right to control your data. You may request deletion of all data associated with your account at any time.

What Data We Delete

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your account information (name, email, business name)
  • All stored OAuth tokens and social media credentials
  • Your posting history and generated captions
  • Any photos stored in our system
  • Client profile and configuration data

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please contact us:

Email: admin@scalesmall.ai

Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

Revoking Platform Access

In addition to requesting data deletion from us, you can immediately revoke our access to your social media accounts:

  • Facebook & Instagram: Go to Settings > Security > Apps and Websites, find Scale Small AI, and remove it.
  • Google (GBP & YouTube): Go to your Google Account > Security > Third-party apps, find Scale Small AI, and remove access.
  • LinkedIn: Go to Settings > Data Privacy > Permitted Services, find Scale Small AI, and remove it.

Contact

If you have questions about data deletion, contact us at admin@scalesmall.ai.
